Usable data

  • controls.thrust (value from (0, 100)%)
  • controls.gimbal (value from (-1.0, 1.0))
  • sensors.altitude (current vertical y position)
  • sensors.position (current horizontal x position (-1.0, 1.0)
  • sensors.angle (current angle in radians; vertical is 0, left is negative, right is positive)
